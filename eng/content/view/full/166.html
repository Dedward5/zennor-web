Project Development

At Zennor we recognise the challenges of developing and running externally funded projects. We have been there so we can empathise. We have unrivalled technical expertise gained by managing funding programmes, delivering over 300 specialist funding workshops and auditing over 200 projects.

One of the biggest difficulties in managing a project is making sure you start off on the right foot. Zennor Consulting can help with a range of practical, tailored support packages to help develop a deliverable project that supports the development of your organisation and/ or partnership.

Our solutions

We can provide tailored support packages including:

  • identifying and researching funding opportunities
  • feasibility studies
  • needs assessments
  • risk assessments
  • budget planning
  • funding advice
  • writing your funding application
  • capacity building and training for bid writing
  • project management
  • partnership development work
  • partner search– regional, national or trans-national
  • funding compliance issues
  • developing project management systems
