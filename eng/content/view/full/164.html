Programme Management Support

Following the 2004 Gershon Review into public sector efficiency, public bodies are increasingly becoming commissioning bodies responsible for the dispersal of funds to deliverers. Zennor can offer unparalleled support to organisations managing funding programmes through a range of bespoke management interventions and staff training.

Zennor’s specialist European programme management expertise makes us ideally placed to help you address programme level issues, we can advise and brief programme management teams and we can help you to support those organisations with whom you have contracts to ensure that they fulfill their contractual obligations whilst achieving their full potential and having the maximum impact on the communities and economies surrounding them.

Our solutions

  • Writing effective tender specifications for programmes
  • Project application appraisals and frameworks
  • Programme management training to Managing Authority staff
  • Development of programme management systems including ICT
  • Creation of internal monitoring procedures
  • Interim management and support in recruiting programme managers
  • Monitoring visits (Article 4)
  • External audit support (Article 10)
  • Audit certification special investigations
  • Programme evaluations
