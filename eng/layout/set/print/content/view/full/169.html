Project Support - Project Management

Effective project management is vital for all types of initiatives. Zennor Consulting provide clear guidance and support to simplify the process. With our help your systems and delivery will comply with all the funders’ requirements so you can focus on delivering the outputs.

We offer a flexible approach so you can choose a mixture of solutions appropriate to your needs. These solutions can be made available on a one to one basis or through group training depending on your requirements.

Our solutions

We can provide tailored support packages including:

  • feasibility studies
  • needs assessments
  • preparing project delivery plans and evaluation frameworks
  • developing project management systems, procedures and protocols
  • partnership development activities
  • assistance with interim, final claims or project closure reports
  • interim management
  • evaluations of the project experience, complete with practical recommendations
  • specialist recruitment support for hiring your own project management staff
  • staff fluent in several languages to help with trans-national project work
