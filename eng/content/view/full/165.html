Learning and Skills

Zennor Consulting works extensively with the Learning and Skills Council, Department for Education and Skills, colleges, work based learning providers, learning partnerships, education business partnerships, schools and universities.

We recognise the challenges the sector faces in developing systems and partnerships that can offer effective progression routes for young people and adults equipped with the skills, competencies and qualifications needed by employers.

Our people are extremely knowledgeable and experienced in a range of learning and skills environments and provide practical yet innovative solutions.

Our solutions

  • identifying and implementing creative and practical solutions
  • developing effective partnership protocols and procedures
  • feasibility studies
  • risk assessments
  • budget planning
  • researching funding opportunities
  • project management support
  • capacity building
  • evaluating education and training programmes
  • verification and audit assistance

Our expertise

  • 14-19 partnership developments
  • project management and support, including European Social Fund
  • evaluation of education and training programmes
  • learning and skills policy development
  • widening participation
  • quantitative and qualitative research
  • training/ capacity building

Our influence

The many organisations that have benefited from our learning and skills work include:

  • Department for Education and Skills (support of Get On campaign)
  • Learning and Skills Council Black Country (ESF project appraisals)
  • Ysgol Hen Felin (Rhondda Special School) (project evaluation)
  • National Arts Learning Network (widening participation research)
