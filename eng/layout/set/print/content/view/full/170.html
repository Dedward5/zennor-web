Project Support Audit & Verification

Zennor Consulting has long experience of helping organisations comply with the audit regulations and guidelines specified by various funding regimes. Our expert audit services provide organisations with considerable assurances of compliance through a range of techniques.

We have successfully supported organisations dealing with ESF, ERDF and INTERREG activity to develop internal and external audit programmes. Our specialised knowledge will especially help organisations within the learning and skills, community development and regeneration sectors.

We work in partnership with Bentley Jennison Chartered Accountants and Europa Audit Services to conduct audit work leading to certification of claims.

Our solutions

We can provide tailored support packages including:

  • Setting up simple, effective record systems
  • Individual project audits – financial and output focused
  • Undertaking record keeping reconstruction work as required
  • Assistance in compiling claims, including interim and final claims
  • High level systems audits
  • Reviewing, and recommending improvements to, financial practices and controls
  • Financial health checks
  • Pre-audit health checks
  • Audit rehearsals
  • External audit certification
